Welcome to StockChain

21st Century Money

StockChain is an investment platform offering the returns of a mutual fund with the flexibility of a standard bank account. StockChain takes the capital provided by investors and reinvests it in stocks and shares. In return, investors are given units of the fund which represent a stake in the stocks and shares investments made by StockChain, just like a standard mutual fund. These units can be traded with other investors freely, differentiating StockChain from other schemes.

Please use this site as you would any other and provide feedback by answering the questions in Part 2 of the google docs questionnaire. Feel free to sign up for an account, add funds and trade with others (all completely free for testing purposes of course).
